Frequently Asked Questions

What is your shipping policy?

We offer worldwide shipping with various options including standard (5-7 business days), express (2-3 business days), and overnight shipping. Shipping costs vary based on destination and weight.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Return shipping is free for defective items. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for salons, beauty stores, and licensed professionals. Please contact our sales team at sales@norahairline.com for more information and pricing.

How do I care for my hair products?

We provide care instructions with every product. Generally, we recommend using sulfate-free shampoos, avoiding excessive heat, and storing hair properly when not in use.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers for wholesale orders.
